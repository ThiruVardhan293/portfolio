My top 3 movies of all time

Bahubali

This movie has set a benchmark for many or the Indian directors who make movies with high budgets like the RAONE and Dhoom 3.
I've never seen such an awesome war scene ever in any of the Hollywood movies even.
That scene is a treat for our eyes and our mind.

Tumbad

Tumbbad's story is also unique. It's more than just a horror film with jump scares.
Instead, it focuses on human nature, societies, casteist norms and much more.
